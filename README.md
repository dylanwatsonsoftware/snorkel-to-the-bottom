# Snorkel to the Bottom

Tech stack:
- npm
- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript
- Phaser 3

Idea:
A web game where you control a pirate snorkeller.
You start on your ship and you need to drive your boat then dive down to the bottom to get treasure.

You might encounter bad pirates and you have to fight them to get the treasure.

You have to get back up to the surface to drop off the treasure.

You can sail across the seven seas to find pirates and take their treasure and sink their boat.

Mermaids can give you crystals and money.
Treasure chests can give you money and crystals.
You can collect scuba tanks to get more air.

Controls:
- Down arrow to swim down
- Up arrow to swim up
- Left arrow to move left
- Right arrow to move right
- Space to shoot

Mechanics:
- You have a limited air supply
- You can collect air bubbles to replenish your air supply
- You can collect treasure chests to increase your score
- There are different types of treasure chests with different values
- There are different types of enemies that will try to stop you
- There are different types of obstacles that will try to stop you
- The game gets progressively harder
- The game ends when you run out of air or hit an enemy or obstacle
